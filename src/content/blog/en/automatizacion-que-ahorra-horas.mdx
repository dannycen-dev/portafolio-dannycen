## The usual mistake

Wiring two apps “because you can” isn’t automation. Automation means **removing repeatable work** without creating ops debt (fragile flows nobody understands).

I use **n8n, Make, and Zapier** depending on the client: on-prem needs, SaaS stack, budget, and available technical staff.

## Short method

1. Observe a real week of operations
2. Separate: what to automate vs. keep human
3. Design the happy path and the failures (timeouts, duplicates, permissions)
4. Document each scenario with a business owner

## Deliverables I often ship

- Lead from form/WhatsApp → CRM → task → closer notification
- Closed-won → invoicing / folder / onboarding
- Daily reports to Slack or email — no hand-built Excel

The KPI I care about: **hours recovered per month** and fewer capture errors.
