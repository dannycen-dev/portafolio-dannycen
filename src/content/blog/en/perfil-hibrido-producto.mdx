## The gap between “the idea” and “it’s in prod”

I’ve been a **Product Owner, Project Manager, and Applications Administrator**. That mix avoids telephone tag: the person who understands the problem also knows how to wire it.

## What it looks like in practice

- Roadmap in business language + technical acceptance criteria
- Prioritization by impact (hours, conversion, risk)
- Short weekly demos — not endless slide decks
- Minimum viable docs so the team can operate

## When this profile fits

Startups and SMBs that need someone to **design the system and leave it running**: CRM, bots, dashboards, flows. Not just another ticket on a third-party backlog.

If “no one between teams closes it” sounds familiar, that’s usually exactly the gap I cover.
