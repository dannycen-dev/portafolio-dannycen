## Why pipelines break

The lead writes on WhatsApp, the rep answers from a phone… and the CRM finds out late (or never). You lose history, metrics, and follow-ups.

As a **certified Kommo implementer** and **WhatsApp Cloud API** integrator, I build the bridge:

- centralized conversations
- tags and pipeline stages
- bots for early qualification
- clean handoff to a human agent

## Healthy go-live checklist

- Numbers and templates approved in Meta
- Pipeline aligned to the real sales process
- Roles and permissions per team
- Conversation backup and basic audit

Expected outcome: fewer “floating” leads and a team working from one board.
