## The project

**Idea2Form** is a Collaborative Design Studio that makes bold causes easy to connect with and impossible to ignore. The site communicates who they are, their design philosophy, and a portfolio for mission-driven work.

## My contribution

[Idris Aguero](https://idrisaguero.com/) designed the **Figma wireframes**. My role was **front-end development**: implementing the studio narrative, Projects / Who we are / Design Philosophy hierarchy, and an experience that supports client collaboration conversations — matching the design where it mattered.


## Approach

- Core message: *design shifts narratives, shapes experiences, and drives engagement*
- Design as mission-critical — not a nice-to-have — for impact organizations
- Blog and contact points to grow relationship with the creative community
