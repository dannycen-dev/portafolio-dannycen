## The project

**Equity Is Dynamic** is Elemental’s home for equity-focused initiatives and resources to redesign the systems at the root of climate change and pursue truly equitable climate action.

## My contribution

[Idris Aguero](https://idrisaguero.com/) / Idea2Form delivered the **Figma wireframes** for the beta interface. I handled **implementation in code**: expressive typography, product framing, and newsletter/updates onboarding — without drifting from the visual system in the design.


## What it communicates

- An introspective tool for climate-tech founders and CEOs
- Equitable practices that strengthen diversity and inclusion in the sector
- An early-adopter community via updates signup
