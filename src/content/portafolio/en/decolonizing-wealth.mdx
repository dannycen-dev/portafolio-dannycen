## The project

**Decolonizing Wealth Project** works on *Transforming Wealth into Collective Wellbeing™*: using wealth as medicine through transformational experiences, impact storytelling, and movement-led grantmaking.

## My contribution

From [Idris Aguero](https://idrisaguero.com/)’s **Figma wireframes**, I **coded** the web presence: purpose hero, impact metrics, who we are / what we do blocks, and the news/funds editorial flow — translating the design into HTML/CSS/JS faithful to the file.


## Product signals

- Visible metrics (~$1B influenced, $30M distributed, since 2018)
- Focus on repairing colonial harm and collective belonging
- Fund updates (Youth Mental Health, Indigenous Earth Fund, California Truth and Healing)
