## The project

The **East Palo Alto Community Archive (EPACA)** is a community-led initiative to collect, share, celebrate, and preserve the unique history of East Palo Alto. The site connects catalog exploration, submissions, donations, and community event storytelling.

## My contribution

[Idris Aguero](https://idrisaguero.com/) / Idea2Form produced the **Figma wireframes**. My job was to **code the site**: turn that design into a live experience faithful to Figma — clear archive exploration flows, editorial hierarchy (news, events, collections), and careful implementation with accessibility and the project’s cultural mission in mind.


## Visible outcomes

- Discovery-led home: explore, contribute, and support the archive
- Sections for updates, events, and community voices
- Resources such as *Small But Mighty* and Ohlone land acknowledgment
- Link to the live catalog at `catalog.epacommunityarchive.org`
