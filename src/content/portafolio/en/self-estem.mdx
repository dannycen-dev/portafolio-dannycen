## The project

**Self-eSTEM** positions women as the future of science, tech, engineering, and math. The platform communicates mission, programs (Engage, Recruit, Reinvest), impact metrics, media, and ways to get involved or donate.

## My contribution

[Idris Aguero](https://idrisaguero.com/) / Idea2Form defined the **Figma wireframes**. I **coded the site** so the design became real: motion-forward hero, impact modules, program CTAs, and Innovator storytelling — respecting typography, spacing, and components from the design file.


## What the product conveys

- Mission: pride, purpose, and possibility through STEM training
- Talent pipeline: early immersion → career sustainment → community
- Tangible impact (innovators, training hours, partnerships)
- Expansion of digital and AI literacy in underserved communities
